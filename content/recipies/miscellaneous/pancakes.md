## Ingredients

- 180g flour
- 6g baking powder
- 6g sugar
- 1g salt

- 210g milk
- 1 egg
- 15g butter

## Methodology

1. Mix dry ingredients
2. Mix the wet ingredients and melted butter
3. Mix the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients
